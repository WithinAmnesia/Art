Warder: Spiritual Energy Wielder, Mail Conventional. Stalwart Spirit Wielder; Utilitarian yet Powerful.

Wanderer: Physical Energy Wielder, Leather Conventional. Mobile Physical Striking; Strategic yet Forceful.

Wizard: Magical Energy Wielder, Cloth Conventional. Intensive Magic Casting; Demanding yet Potent.

Worlder: Physical, Spiritual and Magical Energy Wielder, Leather / Mail Conventional. Warrior, Warder, Wanderer, Wizard together as one entity; a World Wielder.
