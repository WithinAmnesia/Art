AGABAL M.M.O.A.R.P.G. R.T.S. 3 Meters Per Second Run Speed 



3 Meters Per Second Run Speed x 60 Srconds (1 Minute) = 180 Meters Per Minute Travelled (Pairs with 1 AGABAL R.T.S. Minute = 180 Earth Real Life Minutes)
180 Meters Per Minute Travelled x 60 Minutes (1 Hour) = 10,800 Meters Per Hour Travelled or 10.8 Kilometers Per Hour Run Speed
1 Kilometer requires 1,000 Meters / 180 Meters Per Minute Run Speed = ~5.555 (Repeating) Minutes Travelled in a straight line or 'as the crow flies'.
This is ~11-12 Minutes for a round trip or ~7-8 Minutes for a curving path or ~14-16 minutes for a round trip both ways via regular conditions of curving paths.
This ~1 Kilometer x ~1 Kilometer (~1,024 Meters x ~1.024 Meters) is expansive for a video game world major city and or point(s) of interest.
32 Kilometers to 1 Kilometer Scaling works for Points of Interest such as Vancouver City Canada or New York City United States of America.
1,024 Kilometers to 1 Kilmeter Scaling works vast wilderness such as world maps to in game travel distances and in game wilderness expanse.
